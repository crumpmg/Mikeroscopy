MIKE Microscopy

What we do


Mike Microscopy is a novel imaging technology for the life sciences. Desk-top compatible, surface excitation imaging based on IP developed jointly by lksjdf and sdlkfj. Mike Microscopy makes stunning, large-field images in a fraction of the time and cost needed by tradition pathology methods.

Mike Box

The MIKE microscope fits easily into your work space.

Pathology

Processing samples in a fraction of the time with slide-free, nondestructive imaging on fresh or fixed tissue.

Staining

MIKE staining protocols are ultraquick and compatible with standard pathology, immunohistochemistry, and DNA and RNA techniques.

Virtual H&E

Use our realtime color-mapping technology to get H&E like images without the day of waiting.

Texture

Use Mike microscopy to image tissue with a brand-new depth and understanding. Mike microscopy is used throughout the life siences to give researchers a new understanding of tissues and materials.
